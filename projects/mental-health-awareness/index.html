Mental Health Awareness

Your mental health matters. Learn, understand, and take care of yourself.

Anxiety

Anxiety is a feeling of worry or fear that can be mild or intense.

  • Feeling nervous or restless
  • Increased heart rate
  • Difficulty concentrating

Depression

Depression is a mood disorder that causes persistent sadness.

  • Loss of interest
  • Low energy
  • Feeling hopeless

Stress

Stress is the body's response to pressure or challenges.

  • Headaches
  • Sleep problems
  • Irritability

Burnout

Burnout is emotional, mental, and physical exhaustion caused by stress.

  • Feeling drained
  • Lack of motivation
  • Reduced performance

This website is for awareness purposes only and not a medical diagnosis tool.
